June 28, 2022

Long time no updates! But finally, something to write here! Yes, yes - good news!

Our collaborative paper with Dr. Scheler and Benjamin from Dresden was accepted. Look forward to the details soon.

The journey that culminated in this paper started with a simple observation of our Ph.D. student Naoto several years ago. Naoto observed something peculiar in his aggregation experiments on superoxide dismutase 1.

You might just have discarded his observations as "poor reproducibility" or "poor handling of the sample". But no! Naoto was sure that something with the experimental setup had triggered faster protein aggregation in some cases than in other cases.

About five years, a lot of experiments, simulations, and literature study later, we finally began to understand what happened. Anyway, detailed report to follow soon after publication is finalized!
